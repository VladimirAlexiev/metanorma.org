Meet Metanorma

Metanorma is a framework for writing & publishing standards documents with a focus on semantic authoring and flexible output support.

With Metanorma, you can quickly develop tools for standard processing that would conform to the existing norms in your organization.

In the ecosystem of Metanorma-based processos there already exit tools allowing anyone to create compliant ISO, RFC, CalConnect, and some other documents.
