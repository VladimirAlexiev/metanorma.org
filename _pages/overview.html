Why Metanorma?

  • Easy WYSIWYM semantic authoring with syntax based on AsciiDoc.

  • Validation of document contents against a set of style rules, and document structure against a formal schema.

  • Output in HTML, PDF and more with flexibility to tune the rendered documents according to your organization’s needs.

  • Multi-language support.

  • Existing ecosystem of processors compliant with ISO, RFC, CalConnect and other standard body requirements.
