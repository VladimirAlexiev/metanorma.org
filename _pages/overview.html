Why Metanorma?

  • Easy semantic authoring with AsciiDoc-based syntax.

  • Validation of document contents against a set of style rules and its structure against a formal schema.

  • Flexibility in output allows to tune the rendered documents as needed.

  • Multi-language support.

  • Existing ecosystem of processos compliant with ISO, RFC and other standard body requirements.
